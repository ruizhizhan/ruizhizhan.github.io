🎵 **[Music](/life/music/)**: My musical world. Here, I share my journey of learning, playing, and appreciating music.

📷 **[Photography](/life/photography/)**: My visual diary. Armed with my Canon Camera, this is where I document everyday moments and try to capture the fleeting beauty of life as it unfolds.

Take a look around, and I hope you find something interesting.
